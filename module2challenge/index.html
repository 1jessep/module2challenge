About me

Hi my name is Jessep Stynchcomb. Originally from South Carolina, I am currently living in Denton Texas. I am the father of identical twin girls and 2 chihuahuas. In my spare time I enjoy playing videogames, guitar, and spending time with my family doing what needs to be done around the house. I enrolled in SMU Coding Bootcamp because before this I have worked outdoors my entire life and quite frankly I'm sick of earning a living under the summer sun. My wife is a graphic designer and I think we'd make a good team. She suggested that I should give Coding a try so here we are. The ultimate goal is to get hired for a position that allows me to work from home so I'm able to take care of my twin girls. I'd also like to do freelance work. Please take a moment to check out my portfolio and let me know what you think. I look forward to learning so much more.
